Mesut Özil

Player Bio

Mesut Özil is a German professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder. Özil is known for his creativity, vision, and playmaking abilities on the field.

He has played for clubs such as Real Madrid, Arsenal, and Fenerbahçe, and has represented the Germany national team.
